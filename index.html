About

Established in 1964, Sta. Monica Academy is a distinguished Catholic educational institution nestled in Pinamungajan, Cebu. Occupying a serene 0.8-hectare campus, the academy has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and spiritual growth for over five decades. Its enduring commitment to Catholic values and rigorous education has shaped countless professionals and leaders within the community.

With a storied history marked by milestones like its grand 50'th anniversary celebration in December 2014, Sta. Monica Academy continues to thrive as a nurturing haven for learning. Under the capable leadership of Sr. Herminilda B. Ceniza A.R., who serves as the current principal, the academy remains dedicated to its mission of providing high-quality Catholic education. Boasting an enrollment of 458 students in the 2022-2023 school year, the academy offers a vibrant learning environment where students are encouraged to excel academically, spiritually, and socially. Through its steadfast commitment to academic excellence and moral development, Sta. Monica Academy continues to shape the future leaders of tomorrow.

Details

Welcome to Sta. Monica Academy Pinamungajan, where we offer both Junior High School (JHS) and Senior High School (SHS) programs designed to prepare students for higher education and the workforce.

In our SHS program, students can choose from three specialized strands: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL).

Our STEM strand focuses on mathematics, science, and technology, preparing students for careers in STEM-related fields through hands-on experiments and research projects.

The ABM strand provides a foundation in accounting, economics, business management, and entrepreneurship, nurturing future business leaders and entrepreneurs.

Meanwhile, our TVL strand offers practical, skills-based education in various industries, with specialized tracks such as culinary arts, automotive technology, and electronics, preparing students for immediate employment or further education.

At Sta. Monica Academy Pinamungajan, we are dedicated to providing a well-rounded education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences they need to succeed in their chosen paths.

Administrators


Professor Memeothy Dankston

With a passion for both literature and technology, Professor Dankston brings a unique blend of expertise to her classroom. Holding a Master's degree in English Literature and a Bachelor's in Computer Science, he effortlessly intertwines traditional literary analysis with modern digital tools. Before joining academia, Dankston worked in the tech industry as a software developer, where he developed a deep appreciation for the intersection of language and code. His research interests include digital humanities, computational linguistics, and the impact of technology on literature. Outside of teaching, Dankston enjoys hiking, photography, and exploring new cuisines.


Professor Fazzberington

Professor Fazzberington is a dynamic educator with a rich background in history and anthropology. He earned his Ph.D. in Anthropology from Stanford University, specializing in cultural heritage preservation and indigenous studies. Fazzberington's research has taken him to remote corners of the globe, where he has worked closely with indigenous communities to document and preserve their cultural traditions. Prior to his academic career, Fazzberington served as a curator at several prestigious museums, where he curated exhibits on topics ranging from ancient civilizations to contemporary indigenous art. In the classroom, he emphasizes experiential learning and encourages students to engage directly with primary sources. Beyond academia, Fazzberington is an avid traveler, amateur archaeologist, and passionate advocate for cultural diversity.
